About Oscillations

Oscillations is IETE KJSIT's premier annual technical festival, conducted under the esteemed guidelines of IETE-India. This flagship event represents the pinnacle of technical excellence and innovation in our institution.

The festival serves as a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, participate in cutting-edge competitions, and engage with industry experts and academic luminaries.

Under the guidance of IETE-India, Oscillations maintains the highest standards of technical education and provides participants with opportunities to explore emerging technologies, develop practical skills, and network with like-minded innovators.
